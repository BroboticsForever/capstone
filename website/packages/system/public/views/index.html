Brobotics Forever

What are we doing?


Brobotics Forever was started in the summer of 2014 as a personal project with the idea of creating innovative and robust robotic solutions to the problems of the future. We have ideas and projects in progress for development with the Oculus Rift, Quadrocopter drones, smart house devices, android applications and much more. Please register with us to find out more about these projects and to keep up to date with our latest breakthroughs!
